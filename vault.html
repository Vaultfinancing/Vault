Vault
A personal finance dashboard built for clarity — because your finances deserve a calm, organised space to live in.
The idea

Managing personal finances can feel overwhelming — scattered across bank apps, spreadsheets, and mental notes, it's easy to lose the bigger picture. Vault was built to fix that.

The idea is simple: one file, fully offline, that gives you a clear and honest view of where you stand financially. Your assets, your income, your expenses, your debts — all in one place, with projections that help you think ahead rather than just react.

Vault is meant to bring clarity in times when things feel a little chaotic. Not to judge, not to advise — just to show you the truth of your numbers, cleanly.

🔒
Private by default
Everything stays on your computer. No accounts, no cloud, no tracking.
⚡
One file

🎯
Built for clarity

Serkan works in the financial sector and knows firsthand how hard it can be to keep a clear picture of your personal finances — even when you work with numbers every day.

Vault started as a personal tool: something straightforward that could replace the pile of spreadsheets and help organise finances without complexity. The goal was never to build something fancy — just something that works, that you can trust, and that makes you feel in control.

If Vault helps you feel a little more on top of things, it's done its job.
